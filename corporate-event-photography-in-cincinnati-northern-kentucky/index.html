Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you cover?

I photograph conferences, fundraisers, trainings, award ceremonies, leadership summits, corporate retreats, and private business gatherings of all sizes across Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky.

Can you do portraits at events?

Yes.  I can set up a small professional lighting station for on-site headshots or team portraits.  This is a popular add-on for conferences and company celebrations and can be coordinated in advance.

How long will you cover an event?

Coverage is flexible -- from a single hour to multiple days depending on your schedule and needs.  We will discuss timing during your consultation so you receive exactly the coverage you need.

How much does it cost?

Event photography is priced based on coverage time and any add-on services such as on-site headshots.  Contact me for a quick consultation and I will provide a detailed quote tailored to your event.

How quickly will we receive our images?

Most events are delivered within 48 hours.  For time-sensitive needs such as press releases or internal communications, please mention this during your consultation.

How many images do you deliver?

You will receive a curated collection of fully edited images that truly reflect your event.  The final number depends on coverage length, but clients typically receive a generous selection documenting all key moments.

Do you offer commercial usage rights?

Yes.  Your images are delivered with rights for standard business use including marketing, social media, internal communications, and press releases.  Extended licensing is available if needed.

Can you work in low-light venues?

Absolutely.  Corporate events often take place in dim or mixed lighting environments and I am fully equipped to handle these conditions with clean, professional results.
