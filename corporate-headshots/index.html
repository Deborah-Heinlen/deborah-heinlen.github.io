Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take per person?

Individual sessions typically take 5–10 minutes per person, depending on team size and your scheduling needs.  I can photograph 5–10 people per hour.

Do you bring lighting and backgrounds?

Yes.  I bring a full studio-style lighting kit and background options to your location to achieve consistent, professional results for every team member.

Can you match our existing team headshots?

Absolutely.  I can match background, lighting, framing, and overall style so your portraits remain consistent when adding new team members.

What should our team wear?

Solid colors and simple patterns photograph best.  Your team may bring a second outfit if they'd like options.  I'll send wardrobe guidance in advance.

How much does it cost?

Pricing varies based on team size and location.  After a quick phone consultation, I'll prepare a custom quote tailored to your needs — no surprises.

How soon will we receive our images?

Edited images are delivered within 5–7 business days in a private online gallery where your team can easily download their photos.

Do you retouch the photos?

Yes.  All headshots include natural professional retouching for a polished but authentic look that your team will be proud to use.

What licensing is included?

Your team receives licensing for all standard business use including websites, print materials, social media, digital campaigns, and internal communications.
