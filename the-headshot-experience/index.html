Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

My home studio is located in Hebron, Kentucky, just minutes from I-275 and the Cincinnati / Northern Kentucky Airport.

Can you come to my location?

Yes.  On-site headshot sessions and corporate team headshots are available throughout Cincinnati, Northern Kentucky, and the Tri-State region.

How do I prepare for my session?

You'll receive prep guidance on clothing, grooming, and what to bring.  I'll also coach you into natural, confident expressions during your session.

What should I wear?

Simple, solid colors work best.  Avoid busy patterns.  Bring options -- I'll help you choose what photographs best once you arrive.

I don't feel photogenic. Will this still work?

Absolutely.  Expression coaching is a key part of every session.  My job is to help you feel comfortable so your natural confidence comes through on camera.

How do I choose between sessions?

Choose Express for a quick update, Signature for multiple looks, or VIP for a full branding set.  A free phone consult will make the choice clear.

Why not just use my phone or AI?

Professional lighting, posing, expression coaching, and high-end lenses produce results that phone cameras and AI cannot match.  Your headshot represents your reputation.

I'm nervous about being in front of a camera.

That's completely normal.  My goal is to help you feel comfortable from the first minute.  I guide you through every moment so you never feel unsure or awkward.
