Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all Deborah Heinlen Photography LLC ("DHP") websites, applications, and services. This includes all information gathered through our website and services, including information provided in the course of booking a session or exercising your privacy rights.

Prior to using DHP services, please review this policy to ensure you are informed of your rights, our policies and standards, and the regulations that govern your privacy.

DHP's Commitment to Data Privacy

At DHP, the privacy of your data is a top priority. Any data that directly identifies you, such as your name or contact information, or any data that can be reasonably used to identify you, is treated with the utmost care and standard of security.

This notice outlines the ways in which DHP handles your personal data when you interact with our website, services, or personnel in any capacity. It does not apply to third parties or affiliates, or how they may use your personal data.

What is Personal Data?

During the course of our business operations, DHP collects personal information such as your name, contact information, and billing information. DHP collects this data when it is provided through the use of our services, including interacting with our website, booking a photography session, purchasing additional digital images, or signing up for our marketing communications.

Your Privacy Rights and Choices

DHP believes in transparency with our data collection and processing. At any time you are entitled to information regarding what data DHP retains about you, including the categories of data collected, the sources from which it was collected, the purposes for which it is used, and any third parties with whom it is shared.

You have the right to change or correct your information at any time, and to request access, correction, transfer, restriction of processing, or deletion of your personal information. You may also request to be removed from our communications at any time without discriminatory treatment.

Information DHP Collects

Information you provide directly:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Billing and payment information processed through Sprout Studio
  • Communications including emails, messages, and booking inquiries

Information collected automatically through our website:

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on site

How DHP Uses Your Personal Data

DHP uses your personal information to provide photography services, process bookings and payments, deliver digital image galleries, communicate with you about your session, improve our services, and comply with applicable legal requirements. DHP only uses personal data for which it has a lawful basis.

How DHP Shares Your Personal Data

DHP may share your information with the following service providers as necessary to deliver our services:

  • Sprout Studio — for session booking, client management, and payment processing
  • GitHub Pages / Cloudflare — for website hosting and DNS management
  • Google — for analytics and business profile management
  • Email service providers — for client communications

DHP will never sell your personal information to third parties or share it for marketing purposes beyond what is described in this policy.

How DHP Protects Your Personal Data

DHP employs reasonable and appropriate electronic, physical, and operational measures to ensure that your personal information is secure and not subject to unauthorized use, disclosure, modification, or loss.

Children's Data

DHP does not knowingly allow any persons under the age of 18 to register or use its services, and does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

DHP does not directly set cookies on your device. However, certain third-party services embedded in our website — including Google Fonts and our Google Reviews widget — may set their own cookies as part of their standard operation. DHP has no control over these third-party cookies. You may opt out of cookies at any time through your browser settings, or by reviewing the privacy policies of Google and other third-party providers.

Image Storage and Retention

Digital image galleries are hosted through Sprout Studio and are available for client download for the period specified in your session agreement. After that period, images are permanently deleted from all online galleries and Photographer's storage devices. Clients are responsible for maintaining their own backups of downloaded files.

Privacy Regulations

DHP is committed to compliance with applicable data privacy regulations, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other relevant federal and state privacy laws.

Updates to This Policy

DHP reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. An updated version will be posted to this page. Please review this policy periodically to ensure you understand any changes made to it.
