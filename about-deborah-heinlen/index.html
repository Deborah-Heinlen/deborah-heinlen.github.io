My Story

A lifelong connection to photography, and to people.

I grew up with a camera in my hands.  My father was a passionate hobbyist photographer for 65 years, and our house had a darkroom where I learned to develop black and white film as a kid.  Photography has been part of my life for as long as I can remember.  Read about my father's influence on my work.

A couple of years ago, I decided to go professional and specialize in headshots.  Why headshots?  Because I love the moment when someone sees themselves in a photograph with new confidence they've never had before.  That transformation from "I hate having my picture taken" to "Wow, that's really me?" is what I'm here for.

As a member of Peter Hurley's Headshot Crew, I receive ongoing training and mentorship from one of the world's most respected headshot photographers.  This means I'm constantly refining my technique and bringing the best practices to every session.

And here's what might surprise you: I'm also a nurse.  Every day, I care for people during vulnerable and stressful moments.  That same approach carries into my photography.  Whether you're nervous about being in front of the camera or simply want to look your absolute best, I'm here to guide you with patience and encouragement.
