About Deborah

The photographer who makes you forget you're being photographed.

"She made me feel comfortable and at ease."

"It was like taking pictures with an old friend."

"I was nervous at first, but Deborah made it fun."

These are the words clients use most often to describe their headshot experience with me.  And honestly?  That's exactly what I'm going for.

I grew up with a camera in my hands.  My father was a passionate hobbyist photographer, and our house had a darkroom where I learned to develop black and white film as a kid.  Photography has been part of my life for as long as I can remember.

As a member of Peter Hurley's Headshot Crew, I receive ongoing training and mentorship from one of the world's most respected headshot photographers.  And as a nurse, I bring the same patience, calm, and genuine care to every session that I bring to my patients every day.  The result?  Headshots that look natural and confident, because that's exactly how you felt during our session.
